Grammatical Rules:

Capitalizing-Does not exist. All letters are effectively capitalized.

Nouns-refers to an object or person. While names exist, pronouns do not exist.

Past tense-Add sak to the end.

Present tense-Add ki to the end.

Future tense-Add de to the end.

Plural-Add su to the end
