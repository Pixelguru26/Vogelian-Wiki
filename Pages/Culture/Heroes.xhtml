Leader-Supreme Varis Doxaf

Role in the Supremacy

    Varis Doxaf’s role is simple, for he is the leader-supreme of the Vogelian Supremacy. His rule is absolute, and unquestionable, with there being no official check on his power outside of the populace rebelling against him. No one can question him, and he is the sole representative of the nation, with even diplomats being entirely absent. This power is naturally universal, with religion, military, and society all being bound under Varis. He can only be replaced in death, and in death, only those who wrest power can take his role.
